Words That Endure Forever

Wisdom from the Masumeen

Ayara carries the living wisdom of the Ahl al-Bayt — words spoken by the Infallibles that have guided believers across centuries and continents.

Prophet

إِنِّي تَارِكٌ فِيكُمُ الثَّقَلَيْنِ

"I am leaving among you two weighty things: the Book of Allah and my household. Hold fast to them and you shall never go astray."
النَّبِيُّ مُحَمَّد ﷺ Prophet Muhammad · Hadith al-Thaqalayn
Fatimah

شِيعَتُنَا مَنِ اتَّبَعَ أَثَرَنَا

"Our Shia are those who, when alone, remember Allah abundantly, and who follow in our footsteps."
فَاطِمَةُ الزَّهْرَاء عليها السلام Fatimah al-Zahra · Lady of Light · Bihar al-Anwar
Imam I

لَا تَسْتَوْحِشْ طَرِيقَ الْحَقِّ مِنْ قِلَّةِ سَالِكِيهِ

"Do not be lonely on the path of truth because of the scarcity of those who walk it. Do not let your difficulties fill you with anxiety — it is only in the darkest nights that stars shine most brightly."
عَلِيُّ بْنُ أَبِي طَالِب عليه السلام Imam Ali · Commander of the Faithful · Nahj al-Balagha
Imam III

الْمَوْتُ فِي عِزٍّ خَيْرٌ مِنَ الْحَيَاةِ فِي ذُلٍّ

"Death with dignity is better than a life of humiliation. I did not rise to cause mischief, but only to seek reform in my grandfather's nation."
الْحُسَيْنُ بْنُ عَلِي عليه السلام Imam Hussain · Master of Martyrs · Karbala, 61 AH
Imam IV

الصَّمْتُ زَيْنُ الصَّائِمِ وَعِبَادَةُ الصَّامِتِ

"Silence is the adornment of the fasting person and the worship of the silent one. Do not dishonour yourself by making your tongue a vessel for sin."
عَلِيُّ بْنُ الْحُسَيْن عليه السلام Imam Zayn al-Abidin · Sajjad · Sahifah al-Sajjadiyya
Imam VI

الْعِلْمُ سِلَاحٌ وَأَصْلُهُ الْعَقْلُ

"Knowledge is a weapon whose root is the intellect. Acquire it, for it is the means of knowing your duty toward Allah and the companion of the believer in every hardship."
جَعْفَرٌ الصَّادِق عليه السلام Imam al-Sadiq · The Truthful · Usul al-Kafi
Imam VIII

الْإِيمَانُ مَعْرِفَةٌ بِالْقَلْبِ وَإِقْرَارٌ بِاللِّسَانِ وَعَمَلٌ بِالْأَرْكَانِ

"Faith is knowing with the heart, confessing with the tongue, and acting with the limbs. It is these three together that make a believer complete."
عَلِيٌّ الرِّضَا عليه السلام Imam al-Ridha · The Contented · Uyun Akhbar al-Ridha
Imam XII

إِنَّا غَيْرُ مُهْمِلِينَ لِمُرَاعَاتِكُمْ وَلَا نَاسِينَ لِذِكْرِكُمْ

"Surely, we are neither neglectful of your situation nor forgetful of your remembrance. Were it not so, calamity would descend upon you and your enemies would destroy you."
الْمَهْدِيُّ الْمُنْتَظَر عجل الله فرجه Imam al-Mahdi · The Awaited · Tawqi' al-Mahdi
